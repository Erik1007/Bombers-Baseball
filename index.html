Play for Fun

The Bombers are focused on getting better as players and a team. We focus on having fun and the social aspects of the game. We know that we will never be professional players, so we play for fun, not the win.

Convienient Schedule

Most of the players are parents and have difficult schedules. So we play late in the evening once a week to allow everyone playing time that only slightly inconviences family time.
