Welcome All

We welcome players of all skill levels and playing experience. From life long players to first timers, we welcome all that want to play ball.

Convenient

Our field is a quick walk from the metro. Most of the players are parents and have difficult schedules. So we play late in the evening once a week to allow everyone playing time that reduces family time interuption.

Play for Fun

The Bombers are focused on getting better as players and a team. We focus on having fun and the social aspects of the game. We know that we will never be professional players, so we play for fun, not the win.

Social Network

We've got players from all over the world; expand your social network and experience our multicultural diversity.
